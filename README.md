# Primordial Black Hole Evaporation

This repo models Hawking radiation and black hole evaporation over time 

## Files

### 'formula calculations'
Calculates black hole radius, Hawking temperature, and total radiated power based on mass

### 'massradiustempplots'
Creates a table and plots comparing mass, radius, and temperature

### 'timestepmass_nonadaptive'
Simulates mass loss over time using fixed 1-step time steps

### 'mass_vs_time.png'
Plot showing mass decreasing over time for a 1e6 starting mass

### 'massvradius.png'
Plot of black hole radius vs. mass across a range of masses 

### 'temperature_plot.png'
Plot of Hawking temperature vs. mass across a range of masses
